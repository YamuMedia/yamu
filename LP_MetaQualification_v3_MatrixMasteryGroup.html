FAQ

Common questions.

Will this affect my personal credit or HELOC?
Business credit lines structured through MMG's process do not appear on personal credit reports in most cases. For HELOC: this process does not interfere with personal mortgage refinancing or HELOC applications for most clients. We'll confirm the specific answer for your file on the call. How much can I actually expect to get?
The projected range comes from your actual credit file on the call, not a general estimate. Typical range across clients is $50K–$250K. Your specific projection depends on your credit score, existing debt structure, and entity setup. We'll tell you the realistic number before any payment.
A bank already turned me down. Can I still qualify?
Likely yes. Banks evaluate business credit against personal income and W-2 documentation. The lenders in MMG's process evaluate the business entity separately from personal income. A bank decline based on self-employment income does not reflect how these lenders see your profile.
How long does the process take?
The qualification call is scheduled within 24–48 hours of your qualifier form. From the call, most clients receive funded credit lines within 30–60 days. We'll tell you on the call if your file is ready now or needs preparation first — before any fee is paid.
How is this different from other capital stacking firms?
Three structural differences. Flat fee, not a percentage of funded amount. Fee disclosed before you sign, not after. Same named consultants reachable in year three. The BBB record is public year-over-year. We expect you to check it before you book.
What if I do not get the amount I need?
If approvals fall short, we keep working the file at no additional fee. Profile optimization, credit sweep, re-sequencing — all included until the target is reached or we have genuinely exhausted viable paths. We tell you honestly if the latter happens.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. The program fee covers the full advisory process. After year one, clients who want to continue pay a $2,500 annual renewal. No contract beyond the initial engagement. CROA three-day cooling-off applies.
